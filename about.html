But minimalism is a very important functionality — not just a visual trend.

Design is an integral part of the brand's image

Thanks to the design, you can convey the right message to the people and form the desired image.


The Google team conducted the study where was stated that people form their opinion about the site within 0.02 - 0.05 seconds after clicking on the page. This means that the user becomes more loyal to the brand on first visiting.


The same research from the Google team included that visually complex sites are perceived as less beautiful by people. Clean and aesthetic design immediately inspires confidence in people.
