PrinciPill

Branding, identity and system design
Scroll

PrinciPill is a fictitious selection of meal replacement pills that act as a single solution for cooking, cleaning dishes and packing lunches when you are overworked, overwhelmed and have too much on your plate. PrinciPills give you complete nutrition encapsulated in a multitude of flavors quickly and conveniently.

You pick a PrinciPill depending on the time of the meal and their appropriate nutritional value, handpick from a variety of fan-favorite flavors and see your meal come to life.

This project was born out of a desperate need to feed myself something nutritious yet hassle free while chasing deadlines.

Year : 2021

Done in 14 days
